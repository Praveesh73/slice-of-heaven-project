    1. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1 tablespoon olive oil
      • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
      • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
      • 4 eggs
      • Fresh parsley, chopped

      Instructions:

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Brush the dough with olive oil.
      4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the dough.
      5. Distribute the cooked and crumbled bacon over the cheese.
      6. Create small wells in the cheese and bacon, then carefully crack an egg into each well.
      7. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the eggs are set.
      8. Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

      Chef's Note: For a runnier egg yolk, reduce the baking time slightly. Enjoy this delicious breakfast treat!

    2. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1 cup country gravy
      • 1 cup breakfast sausage, cooked and crumbled
      • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
      • 4 eggs

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Spread a layer of country gravy evenly over the dough.
      4. Sprinkle the cooked and crumbled sausage over the gravy.
      5. Add the shredded cheddar cheese on top.
      6. Crack the eggs evenly across the pizza.
      7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the eggs are set.
      8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

      Chef's Note: For an extra kick, sprinkle some black pepper or red chili flakes before serving. Pair with a hot cup of coffee for a complete breakfast experience!


    1. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
      • 1 cup cooked pulled pork
      • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
      • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
      • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Spread 1/2 cup of barbecue sauce evenly over the dough.
      4. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
      5. Distribute the cooked pulled pork and thinly sliced red onion evenly over the cheese.
      6. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
      7. Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

      Chef's Note: For a smoky twist, consider adding a sprinkle of smoked paprika or a drizzle of spicy barbecue sauce before serving. Enjoy your Barbecue Pulled Pork Pizza!

    2. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1 tablespoon olive oil
      • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
      • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
      • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
      • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
      • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Brush the dough with olive oil.
      4. Spread the chopped spinach evenly over the dough.
      5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, Kalamata olives, and thinly sliced red onion on top.
      6. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

      Chef's Note: For an extra burst of flavor, drizzle a bit of balsamic glaze over the pizza before serving. Enjoy your Spinach and Feta Pizza!


    1. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1 cup Alfredo sauce
      • 1 cup cooked chicken, sliced
      • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
      • 1/2 cup spinach, chopped
      • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
      • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, sliced

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Spread 1 cup of Alfredo sauce evenly over the dough.
      4. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
      5. Distribute the sliced cooked chicken, chopped spinach, grated Parmesan cheese, and sliced sun-dried tomatoes evenly over the cheese.
      6. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
      7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

      Chef's Note: Garnish with freshly chopped basil or a drizzle of olive oil for added flavor. Serve with a crisp Caesar salad for a perfect meal!

    2. Ingredients:

      • Pizza dough
      • 1 cup tomato sauce
      • 2 cups fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
      • 4-6 slices prosciutto
      • Fresh arugula
      • Olive oil
      • Balsamic glaze
      • Salt and pepper

      Instructions

      1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
      2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
      3. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
      4. Arrange the fresh mozzarella cheese slices evenly over the sauce.
      5. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
      6. Remove the pizza from the oven and layer the prosciutto slices over the top.
      7. Add a generous handful of fresh arugula on top.
      8. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, and season with salt and pepper before serving.

      Chef's Note: This pizza is best served fresh out of the oven with a side of sparkling water or a light red wine. Customize with your favorite herbs for an extra touch of flavor!
