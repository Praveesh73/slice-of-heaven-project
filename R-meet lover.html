Meat Lover's Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/2 cup ham, diced
  • 1/4 cup cooked ground beef

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Distribute the cooked bacon, Italian sausage, pepperoni, ham, and ground beef evenly over the cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
